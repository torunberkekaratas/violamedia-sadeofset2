Studio · 01

We take our strength
from our values.

For half a century Sade Ofset has printed and built packaging with the same conviction — quietly, precisely, and for the long run.

We continue the honest, fair, human-focused way of working that our founder taught us. The people who run the presses, fold the cartons and check the colour have done so for years; many for decades. We work and serve as a family.

Offset, flexographic and letterpress production all happen under one roof, in our own facility. Nothing leaves the building until it is right — because our name is on it, and has been since 1975.

  • 50years of production
  • 3print technologies in-house
  • 30+countries served
